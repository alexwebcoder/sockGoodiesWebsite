About Us:

Sock Goodies is a monthly subscription service for lovers of one of a kind novelty socks and candy. We pick and deliver fun, exciting socks and the best old time novelty candy for our subscribers. The socks and candy that you receive each month are always a surprise.

Each month, you will receive one pair of new socks and some of your favorite candies. The candy is always fresh and comes sealed in it's original wrapper.

We are based in Los Angeles and are committed to providing you with an awesome experience. Please feel free to give us feedback and let us know how we can improve our service to you.
