Privacy Policy

What information do we collect?

A) When ordering or subscribing from Sockgoodies.com; we collect your name, address, credit card information, email and phone number.

Why is this data collected?

The credit card and shipping information is used for processing your payments and to fulfil and ship your orders. We will only contact you by phone to discuss something related to your order. The email address that you submit is used to send you information and updates pertaining to your order. It is also used to respond to your customer service requests and support needs. It may also be used to send you occasional news about our company. (Every email includes detailed instructions on how to unsubscribe from our email mailings if you wish to do so) Sockgoodies is in compliance with the Online Privacy Protection Act. This means that your information will never be sold, rented, exchanged, given away, swapped or transferred for any reason.

How do we protect your information?

Our security measures keep your personal information safe when you submit your personal information. We use a Verisign verified, highly secure, payment gateway processor called Plug n Pay. Your information is stored on their secure servers and not ours. Whenever you subscribe or order from Sockgoodies.com, you will be taken to Plug N Pay's secure payment gateway processor. This is where you enter in your personal information. You will see an "s" after http in the address bar. You will also see the padlock icon in the address bar. This means that your information is completely secure for your privacy and safety. Your credit card and personal information is transmitted via Secure Socket Layer (SSL) technology and encrypted into our payment gateway provider's database. This high grade encryption process safeguards your personal information and keeps it inaccessible to unauthorized personnel.

The use of Cookies:

We use cookies to keep track of the information in your shopping cart and to compile information about our website traffic. Any providers that help us compile this information are prohibited from using this information in any other way, except to help us process our orders and analyze our web traffic.
